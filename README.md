# Steps to create a basic JUCE plugin template

<div><p><h4>1.</h4> Download and Install JUCE from the <a href="https://juce.com/">JUCE website</a>.</p></div>

<div><p><h4>2.</h4> Launch the projucer app.</p></div>
<img width="741" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 16 13 32" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/0becb96e-39a2-44af-99c3-658367321a06">

<div><p><h4>3.</h4> From the side list, select <b>New Project</b> and select the project type <b>Basic</b> under the <b>Plug-In</b> dropdown.</p></div>
<img width="901" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 16 01 18" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/ad56acbf-9bb9-44e2-9083-2b712d72062e">

<div><p><h4>4.</h4> Create a name for your project.</p></div>
<img width="574" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 16 10 05" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/6250dd90-35b7-4710-8ea0-88ce30ecfd30">

<div><p><h4>5.</h4> Projucer will automatically generate the necessary <a href="https://docs.juce.com/master/topics.html">modules</a> (libraries) based on the project type, additional modules may be selected from the dropdown if required.</p></div>
<img width="576" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 16 10 49" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/0b6cee1a-c2a1-46ae-8e29-e5a5dfd96084">

<div><p><h4>6.</h4> Select the project exporters necessary for your project. The selection of exporters depends on your project's target platforms.</p></div>
<img width="571" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 17 03 52" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/a6962753-f9ef-4106-b1a5-11db969b1a1e">

<div><p><h4>7.</h4> Create your project.</p></div>
<img width="183" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 17 33 34" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/85092ff3-c690-4a61-9f21-5a1b5572a3b5">

<div><p><h4>8.</h4> Under <b>File Explorer</b> new files and resources can be added to your JUCE project. Create new files by clicking the <b>plus</b> icon. Drag in assets or folders to add to your JUCE project.</p></div>
<img width="287" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 17 24 33" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/beeda3df-a1be-4c48-a728-6cdceb4ce1e4">

<div><p><h4>9.</h4> Simarly, additional <b>Modules</b> and <b>Exporters</b> may be added or removed to your JUCE project in the associated dropdown menus. Create new files by clicking the <b>plus</b> icon. Drag in assets or folders to add to your JUCE project.</p></div>
<div style="display: flex;"><img width="200" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 17 30 00" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/b5a511c6-6864-4129-97b8-0e346e27aa2e">
<img width="205" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 17 31 33" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/832f5232-fe72-4bf7-8664-2fc1d54913e8"></div>

<div><p><h4>8.</h4> Open your project in your IDE to start editing</p></div>
<img width="901" alt="Screenshot 2023-10-30 at 17 23 06" src="https://github.com/SolomonMoulangLewis/JUCE-Template/assets/92383851/d4c56509-c45c-43d2-a317-932d6f5721a7">
